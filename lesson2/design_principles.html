Whitespace

FreeCodeCamp

I chose freecodecamp for this princple as they have a very white and clean design. I feel like they are trying to show/tell you how orginzed and easy the website is to navigate.

Alignment

Youtube

I chose the youtube website for it's alignment. I like how the thumbnails are aligned both horazontally and vertically. This makes it easy to navigate with your eyes. Additionally, I feel like it allows for users to find the content they're looking for more quickly.

Rule of Thirds

Typing Club

I chose the typing club website as a good repersenttition for the rule of thirds. This site has in main contents aligned on the left of the website. I feel like this allows the user the know what is the most important feature of this website. Which is a good use of the rule of thirds.
